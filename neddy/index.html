Welcome to My Blog

Here you will find my thoughts and written experience about our major subject Intro to Computing's Lessons and IT Fundamental's. One Topic per Entry. Feel free to read through my entries!

Blog Entry 1

Technology Timeline to Computing's Impact on Industries

This was a group activity where we were tasked to create an infographic about the Integrated Circuit Era, a pivotal period in computing history. The era marked the advent of integrated circuits, which revolutionized technology by miniaturizing and integrating multiple electronic components onto a single chip. This breakthrough led to faster, smaller, and more reliable devices, laying the foundation for modern computing. Collaborating on this project was an engaging experience, allowing us to explore and visually represent the profound impact of this innovation.

Blog Entry 2

Basic Concept of Computer Security

Computer security was one of the major topics in our Introduction to Computing course, where we delved into various aspects of protecting digital systems, including cybercrime cases. For this group activity, we were assigned to research and write about a specific malicious software case. This task deepened our understanding of how cyber threats operate and the importance of robust security measures to counteract them.

Blog Entry 3

HTML and CSS

This was when we first explored and learned about HTML and CSS, with a focus on the basics of HTML. We began with the foundational elements, such as creating headings, paragraphs, lists, and links, which are essential for structuring a webpage. I thoroughly enjoyed diving into these fundamentals and discovering how they come together to build a functional webpage. This activity marked my very first experience with HTML coding, and I’m excited to share both my code and the final outcome with you.

Blog Entry 4

Wireframe Creation

As part of our HTML and CSS lessons, we were tasked with creating a wireframe for our Website Portfolio. Looking back, my initial wireframe and the final website turned out quite different. I decided to deviate from my original plan because building a website proved to be more challenging and complex than I had anticipated, even with a solid grasp of the basics. After some trial and error, I opted for a simpler yet charming design for my portfolio, which I feel suits it perfectly. Overall, I’m happy with how it turned out. Here’s a glimpse of my wireframe!

Blog Entry 5

The Internet and World Wide Web

This was our final lesson for the term, focusing on the Internet and the World Wide Web. For this assignment, we were tasked with conducting a cross-analysis of three emerging trends: Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and the Internet of Things. As a group, we explored the similarities and differences between these technologies and examined their impact on various industries. We also wrote a reflection and conclusion based on our findings. The activity was both informative and enjoyable, and we were able to complete and submit it on time while gaining a deeper understanding of these transformative technologies.

Blog Entry 6

Bouncing ball and Walking Stick Man Animation

We were informed that there would be a collaboration between our Intro to Computing and IT Fundamentals courses, where we would also incorporate what we learned into animation and showcase our works. I must say, I had a lot of fun creating these animations, but I also realized that animating is much harder and more complex than I initially thought. It requires careful attention to detail, ensuring you don't get lost in the frames and maintaining consistency in your drawings for a smooth result. Despite the challenges, we all had a great time working on it. Here are my final output.
